Ingredients:
- 4 tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 10g curry powder
- 10g of royco
- 5g of salt
- 500g of spagatti
- 200ml of water

Directions:

1.  Add oil to the sauce pan. Then add tomatoes and onions.

2.  When they are ready add salt, curry powder and royco.

3.  Add the spagatti and water.

4.  Cook for at least 10 minutes.
